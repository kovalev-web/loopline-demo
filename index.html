Questions people ask

Do we ever need to meet live?

Loopline replaces status meetings, not decisions. Most teams keep one weekly sync and drop the daily standup entirely.

How is this different from Slack?

Slack is a stream; updates get buried in an hour. Loopline threads each person's update by day and project, so it's still findable a month later.
